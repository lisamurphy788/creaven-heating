ABOUT US

Heating and Plumbing Experts in Clare

With over 25 years of experience in the heating & plumbing industry, Ian Creaven Plummers are expert professionals in the field, providing high-quality heating and plumbing services across Clare Limerick Galway Tipperary and the surrounding areas. Our team is fully qualified members of RGI to ensure safety and high standards of workmanship. Our services cover all areas of Munster and Galway bathroom renovations, emergency plumber services, gas & water leak detection services, underfloor heating installation, central heating installation, and more to ensure that your property is safe, warm, and comfortable throughout the year. We provide heating and plumbing services to customers in the domestic, commercial, and industrial sectors, with competitive quotes guaranteed. We work with small and large scale, one off houses and estates.
